Every source we use, in the open.

Tensorust is built only on public-domain or openly licensed records, accessed through official APIs and bulk downloads. No scraping, no paywalled data, no private information. Here is every dataset, its license, and how we get it, so you can check the supply chain behind any number on this site.

How provenance is kept. Every record we collect is stored with the exact source URL it came from and a sha256 hash of the original payload, in a tamper-evident log. That is why any figure on this site can be traced back to the government or publisher response it was parsed from. If you ever find a number you cannot verify at its source, email mattbusel@gmail.com and it will be corrected.
Your data stays yours. These are the only inputs we use. We store no private data, and nothing you type into a tool is sold, shared, or used to train anything.
