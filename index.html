Counterfactual

Test a belief against the public record.

Give the engine a topic and a specific claim. It retrieves source candidates, prioritizes systematic reviews, guidelines, and trials where available, and separately finds public discussions of myths or misconceptions. The live finder does not pretend a search result proves or disproves a claim.

Request the full evidence PDF

Candidate misconceptions

Finds publications that explicitly discuss myths, misconceptions, controversies, or beliefs in the area. Candidate wording is never presented as fact.

Claim-level evidence

Maps a proposed claim to cited source candidates and evidence types, including material that may challenge the claim. Every record links back to PubMed.

Decision-ready PDF

The full Counterfactual Evidence Brief includes up to 30 claims, a claim register, cited evidence and qualification tables, direct PubMed links, and a reviewer decision field.

Live v1 uses public PubMed records. Publication type and search relevance do not establish truth, causality, consensus, or medical guidance. For decision support only, not medical or legal advice.
