Method & trust

Why you can act on it, and verify it in minutes instead of re-checking the work from scratch.
  • Public, permitted data. OpenAlex (CC0), PubMed, USPTO, USAspending, and other open sources, with rights and provenance retained on every record.
  • Traceable claims. Every figure links to its source, so your team verifies instead of trusting.
  • Stated limits. Sampling, disambiguation, and coverage caveats are written into each brief. No overclaiming.
  • No fabrication. We never invent demand, contacts, competitors, or results.
