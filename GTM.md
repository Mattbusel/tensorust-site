# Tensorust GTM — internal

**Not site content.** Nothing in this file should be published to the site. It names
live prospects and contains outreach templates.

## Positioning

> Compete above your headcount.

Tensorust gives challenger companies the evidence capacity of an enterprise research team.

Not another AI assistant. Not a database subscription. A system that finds what larger
competitors missed, connects the evidence, and produces something the customer can
immediately use to win.

## Lead line

> Your competitors have more analysts. They should not know more than you.

Then explain Tensorust:

> Tensorust connects contracts, filings, grants, patents, research, regulation and
> corporate records into cited intelligence that smaller teams can act on immediately.

Strongest supporting line:

> Your experts make the judgment. Tensorust eliminates the days spent finding and
> reconciling the evidence.

## Do not lead with

These explain the machine. They do not explain why anyone should care.

- 200 million records
- AI-powered research
- Data cleaning
- Claim checking
- Replacing analysts
- "The evidence layer for AI"

## Outreach formula

Every message contains four things:

1. Their current competitive fight
2. One thing you discovered
3. Why that discovery could save time, save money, or help them win
4. A small evidence sample they can inspect

### Template — industrial company

> **Subject:** Three public signals about recycled composites your competitors may have missed
>
> Hi [Name],
>
> Your company is competing against manufacturers with much larger regulatory, technical

>
